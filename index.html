My Favourite City : Hyderabad

My favourite city is Hyderabad because its historic forts and Its rich culinary heritage, epitomized by the world-famous Hyderabadi biryani and also consists of people from different cultures.

Must visit places in Hyderabad

  1. Charminar
  2. Golconda fort
  3. Chowmahalal Palace
